FEATURES

Everything you need to navigate the web

Seven powerful capabilities that transform how you interact with any website.

Page Understanding

Converts messy HTML into clear, structured summaries. Know exactly what's on any page instantly.

Voice Navigation

Control any website entirely with your voice. Open, click, scroll, search — all by speaking naturally.

Smart Form Filling

AI identifies form fields and fills them automatically, even when labels are missing or poorly coded.

Page Descriptions

Get natural language descriptions of page layouts. Visual orientation through words.

Intent Actions

Speak your goal, not commands. "Find the cheapest train" triggers search, filter, and comparison automatically.

Safe Confirmation

Critical actions always require your approval. Full control and trust before anything executes.

Issue Detection

Detects accessibility problems on any site. Unlabeled buttons, missing alt text — nothing escapes.
